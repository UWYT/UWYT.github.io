Abous Us!

The Red Insurgency is a Group of Interest (GOI) opposing the SCP Foundation (Although it has been seen opposing factions

such as the Church of the Broken God, the Chaos Insurgency, and the Serpent's Hand). Information about the Red Insurgency is

classified to Level 5 access due to the GOI's intelligence, power, and overall threat. Every SCP it steals and breaches, every

casualty it causes, and every raid started collectively makes its attacks a large step back for the entire Foundation. The GOI

gains its members through each successful raid, as deviating personnel take it as a chance to escape the Foundation.



The Red Insurgency's total member count is [DATA EXPUNGED] as of ██/█/██. One of the greater reasons that this faction is

considered deadly is the way they design their raids. The tactics that this GOI uses ensures chaos, even if it is shut down

quickly. While waging war with other factions, the Red Insurgency has been seen making great use of a facility's ventilation

systems, deploying "juggernauts" (Soldiers in heavy plated armor and miniguns), and using equipment found in past raids. Most

successful raids have been ended willingly by the Red Insurgency forcing cutthroat negotiations.
